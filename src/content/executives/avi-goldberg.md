# Avi Goldberg

**Partner**  
GreatPoint Ventures

**Last Reviewed:** July 17, 2026

## Executive Summary

Avi Goldberg is an entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and technology executive with more than two decades of experience founding, operating, and investing in technology companies. His career spans startup creation, venture development, executive leadership, and venture capital, with a focus on helping emerging companies grow from early-stage concepts into scalable businesses.

## Career Highlights

- Partner at GreatPoint Ventures.
- Founder and former Chief Executive Officer of Dispatch.
- Co-founder and Chief Operating Officer of GreatPoint Energy.
- Co-founder of multiple venture-backed technology companies.
- Active investor, board member, and advisor to emerging technology companies.

## Professional Journey

Goldberg joined GreatPoint Ventures as a Partner in 2022, where he works with early-stage technology companies as an investor and advisor. His responsibilities extend beyond investing to include board participation, strategic guidance, and mentoring founders building high-growth businesses. He also serves as a board observer and advisor to several venture-backed companies.

Before joining GreatPoint Ventures, Goldberg founded Dispatch, serving as Chief Executive Officer before transitioning to a board advisory role. Earlier, he co-founded GreatPoint Energy, where he served as Chief Operating Officer and helped guide the company through significant financing rounds supporting the commercialization of clean energy technologies. His entrepreneurial career also includes founding or co-founding companies in biotechnology, semiconductors, telecommunications, and venture development, several of which were acquired by larger organizations.

Throughout his career, Goldberg has combined operational leadership with venture investing, giving him experience on both sides of company building. His work has focused on identifying promising technologies, assembling leadership teams, raising capital, and helping organizations navigate periods of rapid growth.
