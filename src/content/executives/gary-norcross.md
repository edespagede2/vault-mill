**Former Chairman and Chief Executive Officer**

**Last Reviewed:** July 17, 2026

## Executive Summary

Gary Norcross is a financial technology executive whose career has been dedicated to helping banks, financial institutions, and capital markets firms modernize through technology. Over nearly four decades, he advanced from programming and management roles to become Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of FIS, leading one of the world's largest providers of financial technology solutions and overseeing a global business with approximately $12 billion in annual revenue. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Professional Journey

Norcross began his career in software development and middle management at Systematics before joining Alltel Information Services, where he held leadership roles serving community banks and financial institutions. Those early experiences established a foundation in banking technology and operational leadership that would shape the remainder of his career. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

He joined FIS in 2002 and progressed through a series of increasingly senior executive positions, including President and Chief Operating Officer of the Transaction Processing Services division, Chief Operating Officer, President and Chief Operating Officer, President and Chief Executive Officer, and ultimately Chairman, President, and Chief Executive Officer. This steady progression reflected a long-term leadership role in the company's growth and strategic direction. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

During his tenure as CEO, Norcross led FIS as a Fortune 500 financial technology company serving merchants, banks, and capital markets organizations worldwide. Under his leadership, the company expanded its global scale while focusing on payment processing, banking technology, and capital markets solutions that support the financial services industry. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
