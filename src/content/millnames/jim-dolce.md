# Jim Dolce

Jim Dolce is a technology executive and entrepreneur who has led networking, cloud computing, and cybersecurity companies for more than three decades. He is Chief Executive Officer of Lookout, where he has overseen the company's growth as a provider of cloud-delivered cybersecurity solutions focused on protecting enterprise data, applications, and users.

Dolce became Chief Executive Officer of Lookout in 2014 after building a career leading high-growth technology companies across the networking and communications industries. His experience spans executive leadership, product strategy, business development, strategic partnerships, and go-to-market execution, with a particular emphasis on enterprise software, Software as a Service (SaaS), and cloud computing.

In addition to his executive leadership responsibilities, Dolce has served on the boards of several publicly traded technology companies. He was a member of the Board of Directors at Juniper Networks for more than ten years and previously served on the Board of Directors of Infinera, providing strategic guidance to companies operating in networking, communications infrastructure, and enterprise technology.

Before joining Lookout, Dolce served as Vice President of Carrier Market Development at Akamai Technologies, where he helped expand the company's relationships with communications service providers and strengthen its carrier strategy. Earlier, he served as Chief Executive Officer of Verivue, a content delivery and network infrastructure company that was acquired by Akamai Technologies.

Dolce previously held executive leadership positions at Juniper Networks, where he served as Executive Vice President of Worldwide Field Operations during a period of rapid global expansion. Prior to Juniper, he was Chief Executive Officer of both Unisphere Networks and Redstone Communications, leading organizations focused on advanced networking technologies. Earlier in his career, he served as Vice President at Cascade Communications, one of the pioneering companies in high-performance networking equipment.

Throughout his career, Dolce has consistently focused on helping technology companies scale from emerging businesses into industry leaders. His leadership experience spans enterprise networking, internet infrastructure, cloud services, and cybersecurity, giving him broad perspective across multiple generations of enterprise technology innovation.

He earned a Bachelor of Science in Electronic Computer Engineering from the University of Rhode Island.
