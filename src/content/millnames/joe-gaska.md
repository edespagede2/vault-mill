# Joe Gaska

Joe Gaska is an entrepreneur and technology executive with more than two decades of experience founding, scaling, and leading enterprise software companies. Throughout his career he has specialized in Software as a Service (SaaS), cloud computing, enterprise data management, and go-to-market execution, successfully guiding multiple technology companies from startup formation through acquisition and long-term growth.

Gaska is the Co-Founder and Chief Executive Officer of GRAX, a company focused on helping organizations maximize the long-term value of Salesforce and other SaaS data through advanced backup, archival, compliance, and data management solutions. Since founding the company in 2017, he has led GRAX from an early-stage startup to a profitable enterprise software business serving Global 100 organizations across multiple industries. Under his leadership, the company secured Series A financing from Volition Capital and completed hundreds of enterprise deployments before being acquired by AscendX. His leadership philosophy emphasizes building durable businesses, creating strong company cultures, and maintaining long-term customer success rather than pursuing rapid growth alone.

Before founding GRAX, Gaska served as Head of Sales and Customer Success for Xively following LogMeIn's acquisition of Ionia Corporation. During his tenure he led customer growth initiatives for the Internet of Things (IoT) platform, achieving more than 100 percent year-over-year growth in both revenue and customer acquisition while expanding the organization dramatically. His team also earned numerous industry awards recognizing innovation in IoT technology.

Earlier, Gaska founded Ionia Corporation, an award-winning provider of IoT and logistics software. As Chief Executive Officer he directed the company for nearly a decade, delivering cloud-native SaaS solutions built on AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Salesforce for hundreds of enterprise customers worldwide. The company achieved customer retention exceeding 95 percent before its acquisition by LogMeIn in 2014.

Prior to becoming a technology entrepreneur, Gaska held senior architecture and engineering leadership positions at OneSource Information Services, Digitas, and Elantix Corporation. In these roles he led large-scale infrastructure modernization projects, enterprise software development, global technology deployments, and engineering organizations through periods of rapid growth and corporate acquisitions. His early technical career provided the engineering foundation that later supported his success as both a founder and chief executive.

Across every stage of his career, Gaska has combined deep technical expertise with commercial leadership, enabling him to bridge product development, engineering, customer success, and business strategy. His experience spans startup creation, venture capital fundraising, cloud platforms, enterprise software architecture, mergers and acquisitions, and organizational scaling, making him a recognized leader within the enterprise SaaS ecosystem.

He earned a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Maine at Farmington.
