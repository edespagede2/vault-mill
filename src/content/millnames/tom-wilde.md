# Tom Wilde

Tom Wilde is the Chief Executive Officer of Indico Data, an artificial intelligence company that helps enterprises automate document-intensive business processes using machine learning and intelligent document understanding. Under his leadership, the company has expanded its enterprise AI platform to serve organizations in financial services, insurance, healthcare, and other highly regulated industries.

Wilde has spent more than two decades leading high-growth enterprise software companies focused on data management, artificial intelligence, search technologies, and cloud-based platforms. His career has included executive leadership positions at organizations developing advanced analytics, machine learning, and SaaS solutions for global enterprises.

Prior to joining Indico Data, Wilde served as Chief Executive Officer of RAMP Holdings, where he helped pioneer machine learning technologies for media analytics and content intelligence. Earlier executive leadership roles included serving as Chief Executive Officer of Cxense and holding senior leadership positions within enterprise search and information management businesses.

Throughout his career, Wilde has focused on transforming emerging technologies into scalable commercial platforms. His leadership emphasizes customer success, product innovation, operational execution, and building organizations capable of sustained growth.

His experience spans enterprise AI, cloud software, intelligent automation, predictive analytics, and digital transformation, making him a recognized leader in the evolution of enterprise artificial intelligence.
