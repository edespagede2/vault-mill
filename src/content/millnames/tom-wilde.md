## Professional Journey

Wilde serves as Chief Executive Officer of Indico Data, where he leads the company's strategy and operations as it develops artificial intelligence software for intelligent document processing and enterprise workflow automation. Under his leadership, Indico Data has focused on applying machine learning and generative AI technologies to automate complex business processes within highly regulated industries, including insurance, financial services, healthcare, and government.

Before joining Indico Data, Wilde served as Chief Executive Officer of EveryScape, where he led the company's development of immersive visual mapping and digital twin technologies. Earlier in his career he held executive leadership positions at Rethink Robotics, Microsoft, and SAP, building expertise across enterprise software, cloud platforms, robotics, product management, engineering, and global business operations.

Throughout his career, Wilde has consistently worked at the intersection of emerging technology and commercial execution. His leadership has emphasized building high-performing organizations, developing innovative software platforms, and helping companies navigate periods of rapid technological change.

His experience spans artificial intelligence, enterprise software, cloud computing, robotics, intelligent automation, and data analytics, giving him a broad perspective on how advanced technologies can improve business performance and customer outcomes.
